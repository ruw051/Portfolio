Hand.s

Hand.s is a self-explorating photography project which I contemplated the functionality and underlying meaning of hands in different scenarios. The idea is integrating seemingly unrelated actions performed by hand to tell a comprehensive and layered story of me behind the hands.

Top layer is consisted of a photo of reaching a pokect square and a photo of holding a zip-around wallet. They collectively represent vanity and since cloth realated things are on the surface, they are presented at top.

Second layer is about hobbies. There are four of them and are placed symmetrically with heart shape in the middle. Hobbies are performed often and out of heart's desire, tennis, piano playing, caligraphy and reading are things I like the most.

I think having a shape of a heart is not evident enough and might not be sufficient to exprss the relationship betwee heart and hand so I add a photo of hands posing the heart shape. It is both figurative and real since the photos as a whole is also a heart shape.

The penultimate layer is what I study. On the left is a general representation of coding and on the right is Media in a nutshell. In this presentation the importance as far as I see it, is going downwards, with less important activies on top and more at bottom. Study is crucial in my life and therefore palced at the bottom of my heart.

At the literal bottom of my heart is a Bible with my name imprinted on top. In my opinion it is the foundation of me as a person so it is also presented at the bottom.

The idea of my project is fully explained. Although there are distinct layers, argurably the photos all have equal distance from heart. Hands are distributed, but they are not fragments of who and what I am, instead they weave a complete picture of me as a person.
